ABOUT US
Ceforo is a zestful student based team under the auspices of SRMIST that develops automation technology. We come together to channelize our innovation and knowledge to make a place in our routine life.
MISSION
To build a quintessential co learning environment for students of SRM-IST in the fields of App/WebDev, AI, Robotics, Electronics and Design.
VISION
To incorporate the methodology of automation that can reinforce minimal human interface.

What we are working on

Ceforo Voice

Our own voice assistant has now a basic framework. It has the intelligence of answering who, what, time and weather questions. Our plan is to have it control our various devices too.

AutoCleaner

This AI based device will make your home speckless without any human interference. This pandemic has made us realise the importance of self cleaning robots.

Ceforo Vision

Ceforo Vision is a device inspired by Edith. This device will enable you to see objects far away more clearly, help you make notes without a pen or paper, identifying and tracking objects and much more
